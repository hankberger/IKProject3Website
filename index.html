Inverse Kinematics

Man climbing a rock, rendered in ThreeJS.

Video Demonstration

Features

Single Arm IK

A single arm with a body in the middle to give the illusion of him having 2 arms. 7 links in total.

Joint Limits

Realistic joint limits to give the sense of plausible movment. Toggle button to turn this feature off. [switched off at 0:16]

User Interaction

The goal follows wherever the user is pointing with a mouse. There are also discrete things you can toggle using the UI.

3D Rendering

3D Character, camera, and controls using Three.js. Creative common textures on a plane to give the illusion of a cliff side.

Alternative IK Solver

Ability to switch the solver in real-time using the UI provided.

Technologies Used
